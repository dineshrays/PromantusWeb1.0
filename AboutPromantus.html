About Promantus

Promantus is a team of Technology and Business enthusiasts committed to solving real business challenges to help clients gain competitive advantage by developing actionable strategies to maximize performance and achieve measurable results through the latest Technology solutions. Promantus is a leading IT services provider in the space of Digital Enterprise Solutions, engaged into delivering customized and niche solutions while leveraging the strength of SAP and Workday technologies across the various verticals

We have over 8 years’ experience in managing end to end SAP Consulting and Services implementation experience across multiple SAP solutions. Our offerings span the entire SAP portfolio with a special focus on manufacturing, banking and insurance, automotive, and transport and logistics, we have proven expertise with our solutions and process methodologies.

We also have proven track record in providing consulting and application maintenance services in Workday technology, specialised into integration and testing services.

With our AI based solutions leveraging Advanced Analytics, we have specialized in a wide range of solutions in the areas of IoT, Big Data, Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Predictive Maintenance.

Our Global Delivery Model offers cost-effective and 24x7 services to customers across the globe. We have offices in USA, Europe and India.

At Promantus., we are singularly focused on creating smarter businesses for our clients
