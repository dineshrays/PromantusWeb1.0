PLACER Solution help Transport / Logistics industry

  • Dashboard
      Displays the complete Vehicle list including the parameters such as Vehicle Status, Speed, Ignition & Current Update of data transfer
  • HALT REPORT
      Displays the Vehicle location and its status indication of being Halt(Red)
  • MOVEMENT REPORT (SINGLE DAY)
      Displays the Vehicle route from Source to Destination with respect to Time
  • PRODUCTIVITY REPORT (DAILY)
      Displays the Vehicle’s Total distance, Speed (Max & Average) with separate Movement and Halt durations
  • DHL GEOFENCE REPORT
      Geofence report displays the Entry and Exit time stamp for all vehicles and also calculates the estimation of each vehicle’s stay time inside the premises for all vehicles
